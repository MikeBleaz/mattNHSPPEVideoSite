NHS Covid-19/SARS-CoV2 PPE Guidance

Does the ward you work in have a safe corridor?


Safe corridor: a corridor that connects all the patient rooms and can be closed to patients:
  • All the single occupancy or multi-occupancy rooms have an en-suite toilet and shower. So, patients do not need to use the corridor to get to the toilet or shower
  • The single occupancy or multi-occupancy rooms are separated from the corridor by a door.
