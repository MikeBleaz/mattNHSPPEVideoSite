Emergency Care



Return to start

  2. Donning prior to entering a bay or side room

  4. Put on a fluid resistant surgical face mask
  5. Put on eye protection
  6. Put on gloves
  7. Before entering the bay
  8. Doffing following each patient examination
  9. Don a new set of apron and gloves after each patient
  10. Once round in bay is completed
