Scenario 5 Video:

Out-Patient care



Return to start

  1. Wash hands
  2. Put on a plastic apron
  3. Put on a fluid resistant surgical face mask
  4. Put on eye protection and gloves
  5. Time in high-risk zone
  6. Take off gloves safely
  7. Take off apron safely
  8. Leave room safely
  9. Take off eye protection safely
  10. Clean eye protection
  11. Remove face mask safely
