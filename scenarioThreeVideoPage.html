Scenario 3 Video:

Hospital Ward, No Safe Corridor or rooms have +ve pressure



Return to start

  1. Moving in the unsafe corridor and exiting the ward
  2. Hand hygiene
  3. Donning prior to entering a bay or side room
  4. Don a plastic apron
  5. Put on a fluid resistant surgical face mask
  6. Put on eye protection
  7. Put on gloves
  8. Before entering the bay
  9. Doffing following each patient examination
  10. Don a new set of apron and gloves after each patient
  11. Once round in bay is completed
