NHS Covid-19/SARS-CoV2 PPE Guidance

Guidance for the Safe Use of Personal Protective Equipment (PPE)


When caring for patients with a suspected or proven Covid-19/SARS-CoV2 infection, the level of protection you need depends on the environment you are working in.

This website asks you questions about your working environment and then shows you a video about the correct and safe use of the Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) you must use when caring for Covid-19 patients.
