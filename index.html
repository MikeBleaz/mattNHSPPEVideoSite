Question 1

Does the environment you work in have a safe corridor?

A safe corridor connects all patient rooms and can be closed to patients i.e. they do not need to use the corridor to get to a toilet or shower. This is true where all rooms or bays have en-suite facilities and are separated by door to the "safe corridor". If you are still unsure see the separate section entitled "How do I find out which clinical environment I am working in?"
